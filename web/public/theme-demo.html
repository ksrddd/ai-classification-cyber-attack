Color Philosophy

Dark Theme

Cool slate-navy with a 215° blue undertone — the hue of radar screens and server-room LED wash. Surfaces elevate by getting measurably lighter within the same family: no shadows for depth. Accents are desaturated ~30% from brand to prevent vibrational bleed on dark.

Light Theme

Warm off-white with a 38° paper undertone — quality technical documentation, not a blank monitor. Analysts in daylit rooms get a surface that doesn't bounce light at them. Status signals pop against warm neutral without fighting a hypercontrast white field.

Text Tokens

text-primary — Main reading text. 11.4:1 on bg-base.

text-secondary — Labels, metadata, supporting content. 6.9:1 on bg-base.

text-muted — Eyebrow labels, timestamps. 3.3:1 on bg-base (large text only ≥14px bold).

text-inverse — For text on accent/dark backgrounds.
